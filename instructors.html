swirl

Statistics with Interactive R Learning (An R Package)

You're an instructor!

As you've already learned, swirl is a platform for learning statistics and R simultaneously and interactively. However, an educational platform is only as good as the content it delivers to students. Although we have contributed some content ourselves, swirl is designed in such a way that anyone can create his or her own content to be shared with students everywhere. Whether you are interested in creating a short module or an entire course, you'll find everything you need on this page.

We've tried to make content creation as simple as possible, so that you can focus on the message you wish to convey to students and not nit-picky technical details. The following video walks you through the process of creating a simple module from start to finish:

The video will be right here!

In summary, here are the main steps in creating your own swirl content:

  1. Download both of the following templates (Excel spreadsheets)
    • Module_Info_TEMPLATE.xlsx (Stores information about content)
    • Module_TEMPLATE.xlsx (Stores actual content)
  2. Input module info into Module_Info_TEMPLATE.xlsx spreadsheet
  3. Input module content into Module_TEMPLATE.xlsx spreadsheet
  4. Rename both files (per instructions in video)
  5. Send us your completed spreadsheets so that we can add them to the next release of the swirl package!

If you have any questions about using the swirl platform to share your knowledge with the world (or even just a few people), then please contact us and we'll be happy to help.
