swirl

Statistics with Interactive R Learning (An R Package)

You're an instructor!

As you've already learned, swirl is a platform for learning statistics and R simultaneously and interactively. However, an educational platform is only as good as the content it delivers to students. Although we have contributed some content ourselves, swirl is designed in such a way that anyone can create his or her own content to be shared with students everywhere. Whether you are interested in creating a short module or an entire course, we'd love to hear from you.

Ultimately, you will be provided detailed instructions for content creation on this page. In the meantime, if you'd like to use the swirl platform to share your knowledge with the world (or even just a few people) then please contact us.
